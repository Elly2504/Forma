## Manchester United 2007/08 Home Shirt

One of the most sought-after vintage Nike kits. This is the shirt worn during Manchester United's historic Champions League-winning season under Sir Alex Ferguson.

### Why It's Valuable
- Champions League Final victory (Moscow, 2008)
- Cristiano Ronaldo Ballon d'Or season
- Final season of AIG sponsorship in this design
- Classic Nike template with collar
